Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I place a custom order?

For custom cakes, we recommend 1-2 weeks notice. Wedding cakes and large events should be ordered 4-6 weeks in advance to ensure availability and proper planning time.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We deliver within a 50-mile radius. Delivery fees start at $10 for local deliveries and vary based on distance and order size. Free delivery is available for orders over $200.

Can I schedule a tasting appointment?

Absolutely! We offer complimentary tastings for wedding cake orders over $500. Other tasting sessions are available for $25, which is credited toward your final order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and contactless payments. For large custom orders, we require a 50% deposit with the balance due upon pickup or delivery.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes! We offer gluten-free, vegan, sugar-free, and other specialty dietary options. Please mention any restrictions when placing your order so we can ensure safe preparation.

Can I tour the bakery?

We love sharing our passion for baking! Tours can be arranged during off-peak hours. Please call ahead to schedule a time that works for both you and our baking schedule.

Last-Minute Orders & Emergency Requests

While we always recommend advance ordering, we understand that sometimes you need something special on short notice.
