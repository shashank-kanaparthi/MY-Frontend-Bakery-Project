Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How far in advance should I place my order?

For custom cakes, we recommend at least 1-2 weeks advance notice. For wedding cakes and large events, 4-6 weeks is preferred to ensure availability.

Do you offer tasting sessions?

Yes! We offer complimentary tastings for wedding cake orders over Rs:-650. Additional tasting sessions are available for Rs:-250, which is credited toward your final order.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We offer gluten-free, vegan, sugar-free, and other specialty dietary options. Please discuss your needs during consultation.

What's your cancellation policy?

Orders can be cancelled up to 48 hours before pickup/delivery for a full refund. Custom orders may require longer notice depending on complexity.

Do you deliver outside the city?

Yes, we deliver within a 50-Km radius. Delivery fees vary based on distance and order size. Contact us for specific pricing.

Can I see examples of your work?

Of course! Visit our gallery page to see photos of recent custom cakes and baked goods, or follow us on social media for daily updates.
